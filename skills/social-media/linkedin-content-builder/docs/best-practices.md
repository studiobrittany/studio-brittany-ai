# LINKEDIN BEST PRACTICES & ALGORITHM INSIGHTS

Current best practices based on LinkedIn algorithm behavior (2025).

## UNDERSTANDING THE LINKEDIN ALGORITHM

### HOW CONTENT GETS DISTRIBUTED

**Phase 1: Initial Test (First Hour)**
- LinkedIn shows post to ~10% of network
- Measures: Dwell time, clicks, comments, shares
- Fast engagement signals quality
- Meaningful comments > reactions

**Phase 2: Broader Distribution (Hours 2-24)**
- If Phase 1 performs well, expands reach
- Shows to followers' connections
- Algorithm watches for continued engagement

**Phase 3: Extended Reach (Days 2-7)**
- Top content gets additional boosts
- Can appear in "Trending" or search
- Saves/shares extend content lifespan

### RANKING SIGNALS (IN ORDER)
1. Dwell Time
2. Comment Velocity
3. Engagement Depth (Comments > Shares > Reactions)
4. Poster Authority
5. Content Relevance
6. Recency
7. Connection Strength

### WHAT HURTS PERFORMANCE
- External links in posts
- Suspicious engagement
- Low-quality content
- Inactive profile
- Low engagement rate
- Negative signals (hide, report, unfollow)

## OPTIMAL POSTING STRATEGY

### FREQUENCY
**Recommended**: 3-5 posts per week
- Maintains consistency
- Gives each post time to perform
- Allows quality content creation

### TIMING
**Best Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 5-6 PM (audience timezone)
**Test different times for 2 weeks to find your optimal**

### CONTENT MIX
**80/20 Rule**: 80% value, 20% promotion

**CONTENT PILLARS** (CHOOSE 3-4):
- Educational
- Inspirational
- Personal
- Industry Commentary

## ENGAGEMENT STRATEGIES

### FIRST HOUR IS CRITICAL
1. Post when you can monitor
2. Reply to ALL comments within first hour
3. Engage with 5-10 other posts right after
4. Monitor for negative signals

### RESPONSE STRATEGY
- Reply to every comment
- Ask follow-up questions
- Longer replies signal value
- Tag relevant people when appropriate

## PROFILE OPTIMIZATION

### REQUIRED ELEMENTS
- Professional photo
- Background banner
- Complete headline (220 chars)
- Detailed About section (2,000+ chars)
- Work experience with descriptions
- Education
- Skills (minimum 10)
- Recommendations (at least 3)

### SEO KEYWORDS
Place in:
- Headline
- About section (first 2 lines + throughout)
- Job descriptions
- Skills section

## CONTENT PERFORMANCE METRICS

### ENGAGEMENT RATE
Formula: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Impressions × 100
- Good: 2-5%
- Great: 5-10%
- Viral: 10%+

### TRACK WEEKLY
- Top 3 performing posts
- Bottom 3 posts
- Engagement rate trends
- Follower growth rate

## ADVANCED TACTICS

### SEED COMMENT STRATEGY
Post valuable first comment immediately:
- Additional insights
- Links to resources
- Follow-up question
- Pin this comment

### SERIES STRATEGY
Create connected posts:
- "Part 1 of 3: [Topic]"
- Space parts 2-3 days apart
- Build anticipation

### LINKEDIN ARTICLE STRATEGY
- In-depth tutorials (1,500+ words)
- Thought leadership pieces
- SEO benefits (rank in Google)
- Promote with teaser post

## COMMON MISTAKES

1. Treating LinkedIn like other platforms
2. Being too corporate/formal
3. Over-formatting
4. Ignoring comments
5. No clear value proposition
6. Inconsistent voice
7. Selling too much
8. Not testing

## HASHTAG STRATEGY

### HOW MANY: 3-5 HASHTAGS

### WHICH TYPES:
- 1-2 large (100K-1M followers)
- 2-3 medium (10K-100K followers)
- 0-1 niche (1K-10K followers)

### RESEARCH BEFORE USING
- Click to see active content
- Ensure theme match
- Avoid banned/controversial
