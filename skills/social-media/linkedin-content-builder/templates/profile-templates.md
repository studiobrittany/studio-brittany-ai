# LINKEDIN PROFILE TEMPLATES

Fill-in-the-blank templates for profile optimization.

## HEADLINE TEMPLATES (220 CHARACTERS MAX)

### FORMAT 1: TRANSFORMATION-FOCUSED
```
Helping [target audience] [achieve outcome] | [specialty/method] | [credibility]
```

**Examples**:
- "Helping B2B SaaS Founders Scale to $10M ARR | Growth Marketing | Ex-HubSpot"
- "Helping Solo Entrepreneurs Build 6-Figure Businesses | Digital Products | 3x Founder"

### FORMAT 2: OUTCOME-FIRST
```
[Outcome] for [audience] | [specialty] ‚Üí [value] | [credibility]
```

**Examples**:
- "10X LinkedIn Reach for B2B Brands | Content Strategy ‚Üí Thought Leadership | 500K+ Views"

### FORMAT 3: AUTHORITY-BASED
```
[Role] at [Company] | [What you do] | [Recognition/Results]
```

**Examples**:
- "VP of Marketing at TechCo | Building Category-Defining Brands | 3x Marketing Leader"

### FORMAT 4: SIMPLE & CLEAR
```
[Job Title] | [Specialty Focus] | [Keywords]
```

**Examples**:
- "Product Designer | B2B SaaS | UX/UI, Design Systems, User Research"

### KEYWORDS TO INCLUDE
- Core skill/expertise
- Industry/niche
- Tools/technologies
- Outcomes you deliver
- Target audience

- Face takes 60-70% of frame
- Professional background
- Well-lit (natural light ideal)
- Genuine smile
- Direct eye contact
- High resolution (800x800px)
- Recent (within 2 years)

## CUSTOM URL SETUP

### BEST FORMAT
- FirstnameLastname (e.g., linkedin.com/in/johnsmith)
- FirstnameLastname[Industry] if taken
- FirstnameM.Lastname

### AVOID
- Random numbers
- Nicknames
- Special characters (besides hyphens)
