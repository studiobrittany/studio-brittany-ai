# LinkedIn Carousel Template

Structure for creating high-performing 10-slide carousel posts.

## Optimal Structure: 10 Slides

### Slide 1: Cover Slide
**Purpose**: Hook viewers and promise value

**Elements**:
- Main Title: Bold, benefit-driven
- Subtitle: What they'll learn
- Visual: Eye-catching design
- Your Name/Handle

**Examples**:
- "7 LinkedIn Mistakes Killing Your Reach"
- "The Content Framework That 10X'd My Engagement"

**Design Tips**:
- High contrast background
- Large font (40-60pt for title)
- Brand colors
- Minimal text (10-15 words max)

### Slides 2-9: Content Slides
**Purpose**: Deliver actionable insights

**Structure Per Slide**:
- Header (1 line): Number + insight
- Body (2-4 lines): Explanation/example
- Visual: Supporting graphic

**Content Guidelines**:
- One idea per slide
- 30-50 words per slide
- Active voice
- Specific examples
- Logical build

**Example**:
```
Header: "1. Stop Posting External Links"
Body: "LinkedIn's algorithm suppresses posts with external links.
Instead: Put links in your first comment."
Visual: Icon of link with X
```

### Slide 10: Summary + CTA
**Purpose**: Reinforce takeaways and drive action

**Elements**:
- "Quick Recap:" with 3-5 bullets
- Clear CTA
- Your name and headline
- Follow CTA if appropriate

## Content Patterns

### Educational/Tips Format
1. Cover: "X Tips for [Outcome]"
2-9. Tips (1 per slide)
10. Summary + CTA

### Framework/Process Format
1. Cover: "The [Name] Framework"
2. Why you need this
3-8. Steps (1 per slide)
9. Example/results
10. Summary + CTA

### Mistakes Format
1. Cover: "X Mistakes [Audience] Make"
2. Why these matter
3-8. Mistakes + fixes
9. What to do instead
10. Summary + CTA

## Design Specifications

### Dimensions
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 (square)
- Size: 1080 x 1080 pixels minimum
- Format: PDF (LinkedIn converts to slides)

### Typography
- Title: 40-60pt bold
- Body: 24-32pt
- Minimum: 24pt (mobile readable)
- Max 2 fonts

### Colors
- High contrast backgrounds
- Brand accent colors
- Readable text (WCAG AA)
- Consistent palette

### Mobile Optimization
- Test on mobile first
- Large touch targets
- Readable without zoom

## Tools for Creation
- Canva: Templates, easy
- Figma: More control
- PowerPoint/Keynote: Export as PDF
- Adobe Illustrator: Professional

## Writing the Caption

**Structure**:
1. Hook (2 lines): Tease value
2. Context (1 paragraph): Why it matters
3. Invitation (1 line): Encourage swipe
4. CTA (1 line): What to do after

**Example**:
```
Your LinkedIn posts are invisible.

I analyzed 1,000 top posts to find out why.

These 7 mistakes are killing your reach →

Swipe through for the breakdown.

Which mistake have you been making?
```

## Posting Strategy

### Timing
- Post when audience has time (lunch, evening)
- Avoid rushed morning commute

### Engagement
- Respond to "which slide resonated?" comments
- Reshare after 24 hours
- Pin to Featured section

### Boosting
- First 3 slides previewed - make them count
- Ask "Did you swipe to end?"
- Encourage saves
- Create series for anticipation

## Common Mistakes

**Content**:
❌ Too much text per slide
❌ Not numbered
❌ No clear structure
❌ Weak last slide

**Design**:
❌ Inconsistent styling
❌ Text too small
❌ Low contrast
❌ Generic stock photos

**Strategy**:
❌ Too long (15+ slides)
❌ Not testing what resonates
❌ No CTA
