# LINKEDIN HOOK TEMPLATES

The first 1-2 lines of your post determine if people click "see more." Use these proven patterns.

## PATTERN INTERRUPT HOOKS
- "Everyone's doing [common thing] wrong."
- "I'm about to say something controversial:"
- "Stop [popular advice]."
- "Unpopular opinion:"
- "I used to believe [X]. I was wrong."

## STORY/CONFESSION HOOKS
- "I made a $[X] mistake yesterday."
- "My [role] just quit. Here's what I learned:"
- "I got fired from [company]. Best thing that happened to me."
- "Last week, I [dramatic event]."

## NUMBER/LIST HOOKS
- "X things I wish I knew before [milestone]."
- "I analyzed [number] [things]. Here's what I found:"
- "X years ago, I [starting point]. Today, I [outcome]."

## QUESTION HOOKS
- "What if everything you know about [topic] is wrong?"
- "Want to know the real reason [surprising thing happens]?"
- "Ever wonder why [industry norm exists]?"

## BOLD STATEMENT HOOKS
- "The best [thing] I ever [did/bought/learned]:"
- "[Common thing] is dead."
- "You don't need [popular thing] to [achieve outcome]."

## TRANSFORMATION HOOKS
- "X [time period] ago, I [negative state]. Today, I [positive state]."
- "From [starting point] to [end point] in [timeframe]."
- "I went from [failure] to [success]. Here's how:"

## MYSTERY/CURIOSITY HOOKS
- "Nobody talks about [important thing]."
- "There's a secret to [outcome] that [authority figures] won't tell you."
- "I discovered something about [topic] that changed everything:"

## PAIN POINT HOOKS
- "If your [thing] isn't [working], this is why:"
- "Struggling with [problem]? You're not alone."
- "Your [thing] is failing because of [unexpected reason]."

## CONTRARIAN HOOKS
- "Everything you learned about [topic] is wrong."
- "Hot take: [Popular opinion] is terrible advice."
- "Why [popular strategy] doesn't work anymore:"

## SOCIAL PROOF HOOKS
- "After [achieving milestone], here's what I learned:"
- "I've [done impressive thing] X times. Here's the pattern I see:"
- "I've studied [number] [successful people]. They all do this:"

## HOOK GUIDELINES
**Do**:
- Front-load the most interesting part
- Create curiosity gaps
- Use specific numbers when possible
- Make it personal
- Keep under 120 characters (2 mobile lines)

**Don't**:
- Bury the lede
- Use clickbait that doesn't deliver
- Start with "Hey LinkedIn" or greetings
- Use jargon or buzzwords
