# Changelog

All notable changes to this skill are documented in this file. Format follows [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.0.0/).

## [1.0.0] - 2026-06-26

### Added

- Initial public release
- `SKILL.md` with core workflow, routing table, and quality checklist
- `references/core-keyword-fundamentals.md`: seed keywords, search intent types, matching vs. related terms, clustering, the six core metrics, prioritization scoring, the validation rule, the 2026 tool landscape, common mistakes
- `references/pinterest-keyword-research.md`: Pinterest-specific search behavior, native free tools, third-party tools, keyword placement, keyword bank organization
- `references/blog-website-seo-keywords.md`: macro vs. micro research, focus/secondary keyword model, keyword placement, competitor gap analysis, the zero-traffic reality
- `references/aeo-ai-search-keywords.md`: AEO-specific research approach, funnel-stage keywords, query fan-out, validating AI-brainstormed queries, citability research
- `EXAMPLES.md`: 9 worked scenarios across Pinterest, blog SEO, Etsy, AEO, YouTube, content calendars, competitor analysis, email, and a zero-budget edge case
- `RESEARCH.md`: full source list and verification dates for every statistic and tool claim
- `TESTING-NOTES.md`: validation approach and known limitations
- MIT license

### Notes

All statistics and tool pricing reflect verification as of June 2026. See `RESEARCH.md` for sources and `TESTING-NOTES.md` for known limitations, including one unresolved pricing conflict (Ubersuggest) that is flagged rather than guessed at.
